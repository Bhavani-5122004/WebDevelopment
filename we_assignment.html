    Early Life

    Bhavani Chalasani was born in the Bay Area, California. She moved to Hyderabad, India at the age of nine. She studied in Phoenix Greens School of Learning until 10th class and Dehli Public School, Hyderabad until 12th. She currently studies at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. Growing up, her hobbies included chess and classical dancing. Her current hobbies include baking, reading crime novels and watching thriller movies. She is from Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh and is fluent in both English and Telugu.

    Projects

    Nocturne

    She implemented a website, called Nocturne, which was based off of the idea of IMDB for music. The website allows users to search for songs, preview audio snippets, rate songs, and add songs to their playlist.

    SheGuard

    She worked on the app SheGuard, which is an app that has a button to be pressed whenever a user feels like they're in danger. On pressing the button, all the user's emergency contacts will be alerted and will be sent the user's current location. The user will additionally receive the path to the nearest populated area/police station.

    Language Translation

    She began the implementation of a machine learning model that translates English sentences to French. This is still in progress as of October of 2024.
